    How to play

    Goal

    Be the first to reach 11 points. Play with 2–4 players total — humans and AI bots combined count toward the limit of 4.

    Each round

    • Everyone gets 3 cards from the shared deck.
    • Assign each card to one stat: Power, Speed, or Intelligence.
    • Lock in when ready. When all players lock in, three challenges resolve.

    Challenges

    One challenge runs for each stat. Your card in that slot competes against everyone else’s.

    • Family trump first: Fire beats Metal, Metal beats Earth, Earth beats Water, Water beats Fire.
    • If trump doesn’t decide (same family, or no trump link), the higher stat number wins.
    • Exact ties award no point.
    Fire → Metal Metal → Earth Earth → Water Water → Fire

    Special cards

    Frostpaw Banas (Special) skips family trump. When a Special card is involved, compare the stat numbers instead.

    3–4 players

    Each player challenges the next clockwise neighbor for trump. Survivors then compare stats. Only one winner (or a tie) gets the challenge point.

    Winning

    Win a challenge to gain 1 point. First to 11 wins the game. Review the final round, then see victory.
