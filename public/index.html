How to play
  • 2–4 players. Each round you get 3 cards.
  • Assign one card each to Power, Speed, and Intelligence.
  • Family trump: Fire → Metal → Earth → Water → Fire
  • Special cards (Frostpaw) skip trump — stats decide!
  • Win challenges to score points. First to 11 wins.
